Panchos

Description:

In Argentina we call hotdogs panchos. It's the typical sausage within a bread.

Although you can make it as basic as just a sausage and a bread, it is possible to add almost anything you can think of.

Here I'm bringing you a quite delicious recipe for it.

Ingredients:

    2 German type sausages. 1 pot cheddar spread. 2 white onions. 1 red bell pepper. 3 slices of bacon. 2 tablespoons of butter. 2 tablespoons sugar. Salt and pepper. 2 pancho breads.
