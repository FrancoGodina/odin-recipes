Guiso de arroz

Description:

The guiso de arroz is a recipe that is passed down familiarly.

Although there is a basic recipe, it is fun to add other vegetables or herbs and spices; gently modifying the typical flavors of yellow rice.

We can say that the preparation time is ten minutes and that it takes forty minutes to cook.

Ingredients:

    180 grams of rice. 200 grams of chicken breast. 1 large onion. 1 clove garlic. 1 red bell pepper. 1 green bell pepper. 30 ml of concentrated tomato or 1 peeled tomato. 120 ml of white wine. 750 ml of chicken broth. Saffron in threads. 50 grams of Chickpeas. Salt and pepper.

Steps:

For a better organization, it is advisable to prepare all the ingredients before starting the kitchen task.

Wash the vegetables.

Chop the onion and garlic finely. Reserve.

Remove the seeds from the bell peppers and cut them into small squares. Reserve.

Dry the breast with kitchen paper. Cut into medium cubes - approximately one bite. Reserve.

Heat the broth and keep it warm.

Heat a saucepan and place a bottom of olive oil. Fry the onion with a pinch of salt. Hold the fire strong.

Add the garlic and cook for two minutes, stir so it does not burn.

Add the bell peppers and cook for between six and eight minutes, over moderate heat to prevent them from sticking to the bottom of the pot.

Add the chicken. Mix carefully to integrate the ingredients. Season with salt and pepper lightly.

Saut√© the preparation over a higher heat so that the cooking is even and has a good color.

Add the blended or pureed tomato. Cook three more minutes.

Add the wine. Mix well. Lower the heat level and continue cooking so that the wine evaporates.

Add the rice and cook for two or three minutes all together, stirring well.

Cover with the broth and add the saffron, a dash of lemon juice and thyme to taste.

Maintain the minimum heat and continue cooking slowly, another ten or fifteen minutes.

Constantly monitor the level of the broth to prevent it from drying out excessively. Stir gently from time to time. At the end, add the chickpeas.

When the rice is almost ready, remove from the heat. Let stand for five minutes with the saucepan covered.

Take to the table and enjoy the aroma and special flavor of the yellow rice stew.
